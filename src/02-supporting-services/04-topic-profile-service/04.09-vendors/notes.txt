Required tools/dependencies:
- Vendor record types for contact, cost, stage, and pending follow-up status.
- Private-thread ownership for the adult managing the vendor relationship.

Recommended tools/dependencies:
- Fixtures for quote tracking, follow-up reminders, and completed vendor history.
- Tests that keep outbound behavior minimal unless something is flagged pending.

Technologies and integrations:
- State: VendorsState stored in SQLite (state service) — vendor records with jobs, costs, status, and contact info.
- Follow-up reminders: BullMQ delayed jobs when a vendor job is marked as awaiting_response — fire after a configured quiet period to remind the managing adult.
- Historical queries: SQLite indexed by vendor name and job date so "who fixed the dishwasher last time?" resolves quickly.
- Silent storage: Most vendor interactions are stored silently (Store path) — no proactive outbound unless a job is flagged pending.
- LLM tone shaping: Businesslike, concise tone injected into Claude API prompt.
- No external CRM: All vendor data is internally managed; no third-party CRM or contractor platform integration.
