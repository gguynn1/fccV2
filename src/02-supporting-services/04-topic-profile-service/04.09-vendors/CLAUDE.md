# Vendors

Contractor and service provider tracking. Who did the work, what it cost, what stage it's in, whether follow-up is needed.

Routes to whichever adult is managing the vendor, in their private thread.

The assistant is businesslike here. It stores the record so you can ask "who fixed the dishwasher last time and what did we pay?" months later.

Initiative is follow-up-driven: if a vendor said they'd get back to you and hasn't, the assistant can remind you to chase it down. No proactive outbound unless something is flagged as pending.

Cross-topic connections: Finances (vendor costs log as expenses), Maintenance (vendor work relates to maintenance items).
