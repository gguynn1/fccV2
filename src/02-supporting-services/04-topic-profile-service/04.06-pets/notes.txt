Required tools/dependencies:
- Pet-care profile types for vet visits, medications, grooming, boarding, and shared awareness.
- Responsible-adult mapping so care reminders reach the right thread.

Recommended tools/dependencies:
- Fixtures for overdue care, travel prep, and medication tracking.
- Tests that keep tone warm, practical, and non-escalatory.

Technologies and integrations:
- State: PetsState stored in SQLite (state service) — care log entries, vet history, medications, and grooming records per pet.
- Overdue care detection: Pure logic — compare last care event (vet visit, grooming, etc.) against recommended intervals; surface when overdue.
- Travel-linked checklists: Cross-topic connection to travel — when a trip is planned, generate pet boarding/sitting checklist items.
- Medication tracking: Reuse the same BullMQ repeatable job pattern as health medications if pet meds are configured.
- Responsible-adult routing: Pet concerns route to the adult(s) listed in the pet's routes_to field from the identity service.
- LLM tone shaping: Warm, practical, caretaker tone injected into Claude API prompt.
- No external integrations: All pet data is manually entered; no vet portal API.
