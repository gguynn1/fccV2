# Business

Lead and inquiry tracking for any entity's service business. New inquiries, follow-up timing, draft replies, booking status.

Each entity with a business has a profile that declares their business type and name. The schema is the same regardless of business type — leads, pipeline stages, draft communications, follow-up cycles. The assistant adapts its tone based on the business type.

Routes to the business owner's private thread.

The assistant is professional and organized here — like a capable business assistant. It tracks where each lead is in the pipeline, drafts responses for review, and nudges when a lead has gone quiet too long.

Initiative is pipeline-driven: new lead alerts, follow-up reminders after a configured quiet period, and draft replies staged for the business owner's approval before sending. The tone of drafted client messages adapts to the business type — professional and warm for client-facing services, organized and specific for service-based businesses. Draft communications use Anthropic Claude API, with tone constrained by the entity's `business_type`.

Cross-topic connections: Finances (business income/expense tracking), Calendar (business appointments).
