# Maintenance

Home, vehicle, and appliance maintenance tracking. What needs doing, when it was last done, when it's due next, and what it cost.

Routes to the responsible adult's private thread for individual items, or a shared thread for household-wide maintenance.

The assistant is practical and reminder-driven here. It tracks maintenance cycles so the family doesn't have to remember when something was last done. "Oil change was 4 months ago — next one due around June." "Furnace filter is overdue by 2 weeks."

Initiative is cycle-driven: reminders surface when maintenance is due based on the last-performed date and the configured interval. No nagging on items that aren't overdue. The assistant logs what was done, what it cost, and who handled it — building a maintenance history the family can reference later.

Cross-topic connections: Vendors (maintenance work involves service providers), Finances (maintenance costs log as expenses), Calendar (scheduled maintenance creates calendar events).
