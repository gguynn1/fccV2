Technologies and integrations (documented, not runtime):
- Queue engine: BullMQ with Redis — single queue accepting items from transport/classifier pipeline, data ingest, and scheduler.
- Consumer model: BullMQ worker with concurrency=1 — one item at a time, sequential processing.
- Item contract: PendingQueueItem with source, entity, thread, topic, intent, timestamp — unified across all producers.
- Failed items: BullMQ dead-letter queue for items that fail processing after configured retries.
