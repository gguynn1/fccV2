Technologies and integrations (documented, not runtime):
- Inbound path: Twilio webhook → Fastify endpoint → normalize into InputRecognition types.
- Text/structured choice: Parsed directly from SMS body.
- Reactions: Inferred from short reply content (Twilio SMS does not support native reactions).
- Images/attachments: Twilio MMS media URLs downloaded and passed to Claude API vision for content extraction.
- Forwarded content: Parsed from message body; Claude API extracts structured details (appointment, vendor quote, etc.).
- Silence detection: Timer-based — absence of reply within escalation window; handled by BullMQ delayed jobs.
