Technologies and integrations (documented, not runtime):
- Scheduler engine: BullMQ repeatable jobs with cron expressions for recurring events (morning digest, evening check-in).
- One-off timers: BullMQ delayed jobs for reminder deadlines, follow-up windows, and escalation step transitions.
- Daily rhythm: Cron-based — morning digest (~7am), daytime quiet, evening check-in (~8pm), default silent.
- Queue production: When a trigger fires, the scheduler creates a PendingQueueItem tagged as QueueItemSource.ScheduledTrigger and enqueues via BullMQ.
