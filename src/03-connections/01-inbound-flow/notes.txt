Technologies and integrations (documented, not runtime):
- Phone → Transport: Twilio webhooks deliver inbound SMS/MMS events to a Fastify HTTP endpoint.
- External → Ingest: imapflow (IMAP) for email monitoring; calendar data enters via email parsing or conversational input (no external calendar API).
- Scheduler → Queue: BullMQ repeatable/delayed jobs fire and produce PendingQueueItem entries.
- All three paths converge on the same BullMQ queue with source tagging (QueueItemSource).
