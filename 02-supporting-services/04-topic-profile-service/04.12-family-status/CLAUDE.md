# Family Status

ETA updates, location check-ins, general "who's where and when."

Routes to whatever thread fits the audience — a personal ETA to one person goes in a shared thread with that person, a general family update goes in the family thread.

The assistant is brief and functional here. "PARTICIPANT 1 will be home around 6." It maintains a current snapshot that anyone can ask for, and old entries expire so the picture stays fresh.

Initiative is minimal: the assistant might ask for an ETA update if something calendar-related suggests someone should be in transit, but mostly it just records what people volunteer and makes it available on request.
